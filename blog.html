How Hackers Hack Accounts (And How to Stay Safe)

Cybersecurity is crucial in today's digital world. Hackers use various techniques to gain unauthorized access to accounts. This blog explains some of the common methods and how to protect yourself.

1. Phishing Attacks

Phishing is when attackers trick users into providing their login details via fake emails or websites.

2. Brute Force Attacks

In this method, hackers try multiple password combinations until they find the right one.

3. Keyloggers

Keyloggers are malware that records everything you type, including passwords.

4. Social Engineering

Hackers manipulate people into revealing sensitive information through deception.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Be cautious of suspicious emails
  • Keep your devices and software updated
